### With data-content

```tsx live
<Steps color="neutral">
  <Steps.Item data-content="?">Step 1</Steps.Item>
  <Steps.Item data-content="!">Step 2</Steps.Item>
  <Steps.Item data-content="✓">Step 3</Steps.Item>
  <Steps.Item data-content="✕">Step 4</Steps.Item>
  <Steps.Item data-content="★">Step 5</Steps.Item>
  <Steps.Item data-content="">Step 6</Steps.Item>
  <Steps.Item data-content="●">Step 7</Steps.Item>
</Steps>
```

### Custom colors

```tsx live
<Steps>
  <Steps.Item color="info">Fly to moon</Steps.Item>
  <Steps.Item color="info">Shrink the moon</Steps.Item>
  <Steps.Item color="info">Grab the moon</Steps.Item>
  <Steps.Item color="error" data-content="?">
    Sit on toilet
  </Steps.Item>
</Steps>
```
