---
title: Hero
description: Hero is a component for displaying a large box or image with a title and description.
---

## Examples

### Hero

```tsx live
<Hero className="bg-base-200 min-h-[500px]">
  <Hero.Content className="text-center">
    <div className="max-w-md">
      <h1 className="text-5xl font-bold">Hello there</h1>
      <p className="py-6">
        Provident cupiditate voluptatem et in. Quaerat fugiat ut assumenda
        excepturi exercitationem quasi. In deleniti eaque aut repudiandae et a
        id nisi.
      </p>
      <Button color="primary">Get Started</Button>
    </div>
  </Hero.Content>
</Hero>
```

### Hero with form

```tsx live
<Hero className="bg-base-200 min-h-[500px]">
  <Hero.Content className="flex-col lg:flex-row-reverse">
    <div className="text-center lg:text-left">
      <h1 className="text-5xl font-bold">Login now!</h1>
      <p className="py-6">
        Provident cupiditate voluptatem et in. Quaerat fugiat ut assumenda
        excepturi exercitationem quasi. In deleniti eaque aut repudiandae et a
        id nisi.
      </p>
    </div>
    <Card className="shrink-0 w-full max-w-sm">
      <Card.Body>
        <Form.Control label="Email">
          <Input type="email" placeholder="email" />
        </Form.Control>
        <Form.Control
          label="Password"
          labelBL={<Link hover>Forgot password?</Link>}
        >
          <Input
            autoComplete="new-password"
            type="password"
            placeholder="password"
          />
        </Form.Control>
        <Form.Control className="mt-6">
          <Button color="primary">Login</Button>
        </Form.Control>
      </Card.Body>
    </Card>
  </Hero.Content>
</Hero>
```
